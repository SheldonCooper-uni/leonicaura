FAQFrequently asked questions

Here are answers to common questions.

How long is a lesson?

A lesson usually lasts 60 minutes. The duration can be adjusted by agreement.

How does payment work?

You can pay after each lesson via PayPal or bank transfer — no contract required.

What happens with short-notice cancellations?

You can cancel or reschedule for free up to 24 hours before the appointment. Later cancellations are charged.

Which platform do you use for lessons?

I teach via Google Meet or Zoom. You’ll receive a link before the lesson by email or WhatsApp.

Are homework assignments required?

Homework is optional, but it helps you reinforce what you’ve learned.

Can I message you late at night?

Yes! You can message me anytime — even at 22:00 or 00:00. I reply as quickly as possible, and I’m often online at those times. Late messages are totally fine.

What exactly are your working hours?

Monday to Thursday: 17:00–22:00 (latest start 22:00), Friday: 15:00–22:00 (latest start 22:00), Saturday and Sunday: 06:00–22:00. During NRW school holidays I’m available 06:00–22:00.

Do you offer double or triple sessions?

Yes! You can book 2 or 3 hours in a row if you want to learn more intensively. This is popular for exam prep or during holidays.

How do I book 5+ hours and how long are they valid?

You can book multiple lessons at once (e.g., 5 or 10 hours) and use them flexibly. For 5+ hours the price is 13 €/h, for 10+ hours it’s 10 €/h.
